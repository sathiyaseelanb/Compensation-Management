Executive Education Brief

This simulation asks participants to sort compensation elements first by direct versus indirect compensation, then by their role in a Total Rewards framework.

The exercise is intentionally precise. The goal is not to memorize labels, but to see how classification choices influence pay philosophy, budgeting, communication, and governance.

Learning Objectives

  • Distinguish direct compensation from indirect compensation.
  • Classify reward elements into salary, allowances, incentives, benefits, and recognition.
  • Connect individual reward elements to the broader architecture of Total Rewards.
