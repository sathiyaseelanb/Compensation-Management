Classify the Forms of Pay.

A two-stage classification exercise on the architecture of Total Rewards. Sort thirty-nine compensation elements first by Direct versus Indirect compensation, then into the six-part Total Rewards framework. There is no perfect score — only disciplined judgement.

Learning Objectives

Objective 01 Distinguish direct compensation from indirect compensation across the full pay landscape.
Objective 02 Classify reward elements into base salary, allowances, incentives, benefits, and recognition.
Objective 03 Connect individual reward elements to the broader architecture of Total Rewards.

How to classify

Tap any card in the Compensation Elements panel to select it. The card turns red and the destination zones light up.

Tap a zone to drop the selected card into that category. You can move placed cards the same way — tap to re-select, then tap a different zone.

Tap the small "i" on any card to open its concept card — definition, why it matters, an example, and a reflective question. Read before you classify; the cards are designed to teach.
